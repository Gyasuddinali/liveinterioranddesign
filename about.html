About Us

Who We Are

At Live Interior & Design , is a creative interior design company established in 2020, dedicated to transforming spaces into functional, elegant, and inspiring environments. We believe that great design is not just about aesthetics—it’s about creating spaces that reflect personality, enhance comfort, and improve the way people live and work. Since our inception, we have focused on delivering thoughtful design solutions tailored to our clients’ needs, lifestyles, and budgets. From residential interiors to commercial spaces, our team combines creativity, attention to detail, and practical expertise to bring every vision to life. At Live Interior Design, we are committed to quality craftsmanship, innovative ideas, and client satisfaction. We work closely with our clients at every stage—from concept to completion—to ensure each project is unique, timeless, and perfectly aligned with their expectations.

Our Mission

At Live Interior & Design, our mission is to create meaningful interior spaces that combine aesthetics, functionality, and comfort. We strive to transform homes, offices, and commercial environments through innovative design solutions, quality craftsmanship, and attention to detail. Our goal is to understand each client’s vision and deliver customized interiors that reflect their lifestyle, brand, and purpose. We are committed to using high-quality materials, modern techniques, and smart space planning to ensure long-lasting value. By maintaining transparency, timely execution, and customer-centric service, we aim to exceed expectations and build lasting relationships. At Live Interior & Design, our mission is to design spaces that truly come alive and enhance everyday living and working experiences

Our Vision

At Live Interior & Design, our vision is to create inspiring interior spaces that seamlessly blend creativity, functionality, and lasting quality. We aim to design environments that enhance everyday living and working experiences while reflecting individuality and purpose. We strive to become a trusted interior design brand by delivering innovative, sustainable, and well-crafted solutions for residential and commercial spaces. Through continuous learning and modern design practices, we set new standards in excellence. Our vision is to build long-term relationships with our clients based on transparency, professionalism, and trust, while transforming spaces that truly feel alive and meaningful.
